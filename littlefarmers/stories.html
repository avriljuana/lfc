Brewing a brighter future for quality local coffee

The time has come to change the stereotype of the term local. It happens too often that people have associated “local” to negative categories such as low quality, inferior, or cheap—stuck on the idea that foreign goods and imported brands will always be better. But that is no longer the case. Better mornings are guaranteed for the agricultural industry in every cup of locally grown coffee. The moment Little Farmers Coffee Brim planted the roots of their first store in Bacolod, it began cultivating perfect conditions for Filipino coffee awareness and loving local.
