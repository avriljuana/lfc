Our Heritage

Rooted in Philippine soil, crafted for every cup.

Little Farmers Coffee is a shop that specializes in the wholesale and retail of fresh roasted coffee beans and seeks to be the spark and current that fans the flames of awareness for Filipino coffee.

“Little Farmers Coffee are coffee beans from all over the Philippines,” explains Nicky and Dayle Banaag, owners of Little Farmers Coffee Brim.

“We have several blends of coffee beans, gathered from the highlands, roasted in different blends to cater the palate of every customer.”

With a patriotic drive to encourage demand for Philippine coffee, owners Nicky and Dayle began embedding the roots of their advocacy deeper as they began to open more branches.

“Due to the commendable recognition of the market,” they tell, “It was then decided to set up a retail environment to cater to customer’s personal consumptions.”

As the traction for their unique concept picked up and grew along with likeminded businesses in the country, so did the local demand for coffee.
